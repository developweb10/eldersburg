About

Hours by Appointment Only

Eldersburg Armory is a Homebased Federal and State Licensed Gun Shop located in the 21784 Zip code. This shop is first responder owned and operated. Working on three years in business we are rapidly growing to serve the needs of the community and those surrounding areas. Founded in late 2020 and becoming operational in early 2021 we've seen a lot of faces come through the door.

From helping with your first purchase, to answering questions, sales, consignment, trade-in's and raffles. There are many way's we have the potential to earn your business. Over our time of operation we have seen many of firearms, and have access to most items within this industry. All it takes is a phone call, email, or online purchase to get your foot in the door!
